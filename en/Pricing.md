Note: You salesperson will walk you through paying the fee and help you get your APPID in one working day. Only valid APPID has access to eWeLink cloud platform service.

- Cloud cloud docking: OAuth2.0 Authorized login method, temporarily free, including 1 interface call certificate valid for 1 year, no technical support, limited documentation, limited number of interfaces, and limited call frequency.
